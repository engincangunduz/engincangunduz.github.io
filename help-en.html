What is PrayerApp?

PrayerApp is a simple and user-friendly mobile application designed to support daily worship. It provides prayer times, Qibla direction, daily verses, religious days and reminders in one place.

Our goal is to create a fast, easy-to-understand and comfortable experience for users of all ages. The application continues to improve regularly and user feedback plays an important role in this process.

What Does the App Offer?

Prayer Times

You can view daily prayer times according to your location or the city you select.

Iftar / Suhoor Time

Especially during Ramadan, you can easily track the remaining time until iftar or suhoor.

Qibla Compass

Using your device sensors, you can determine the Qibla direction easily.

Daily Verse

You can read the daily verse and follow inspirational religious content.

Religious Days

Access important Islamic nights, holidays and other religious dates easily.

Notifications

You can receive reminders for prayer times and other important events.

How Prayer Times Work

Prayer times are calculated according to your selected location and calculation method. If automatic location is enabled, your device location will be used.

Different institutions may use slightly different calculation methods, which may cause small variations in prayer times.

Important Note Prayer times may vary depending on location, device settings and calculation methods.

About the Qibla Feature

The Qibla compass uses your device sensors to determine the direction of Mecca. External magnetic interference may affect accuracy.

For best results keep your phone flat and avoid metallic objects nearby.

Notifications

Notifications help remind you of prayer times and important moments. Make sure notifications are enabled in both the app and iPhone settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are prayer times different?

Different calculation methods or incorrect location settings may cause this.

Why is my location incorrect?

Disable automatic location and manually select your city if needed.

Why does the Qibla direction change?

Device sensors can be affected by magnetic interference or metal surfaces.

Why are notifications not appearing?

Check both the app notification settings and your iPhone notification permissions.
