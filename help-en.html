About PrayerApp

PrayerApp is a simple and practical mobile application designed to help Muslims keep track of daily prayer times and other useful religious information.

The application includes prayer times, Qibla direction, daily verses, religious days and helpful reminders. Our goal is to provide a fast, clean and easy-to-use experience for everyone.

Prayer Times

Prayer times are calculated based on your selected location and calculation method. If automatic location is enabled, the app will use your device location. Otherwise you can manually choose a city.

Different regions may use slightly different calculation methods. You can change the calculation method from the Settings section.

Qibla Compass

The Qibla compass uses your device sensors to determine the direction of Mecca. For the best accuracy, keep your phone flat and away from metal objects or magnetic interference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are prayer times different?

This usually happens due to different calculation methods or incorrect location settings.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled both inside the app and in your iPhone system settings.

Does the app work offline?

Some information may still appear offline, but updated prayer times and certain features require internet access.
