About

Hello 👋🏻 Our story started years ago as a university software project. When we first released the Prayer Times application, there were not many similar apps available. Our goal was to help users easily track prayer times and set alarms for any prayer time directly from their phones.

Today we continue improving our applications with the same excitement we had on the first day. Along with Prayer Times, Iftara Ne Kadar Kaldı and our upcoming applications, we aim to deliver carefully designed digital products to more Muslims around the world.

We take great pride in the products we build and continuously work to improve them based on user feedback. Messages shared through the app and emails sent to us are carefully reviewed so we can make our applications more useful and user-friendly.

Thank you for your feedback and support. We wish you peaceful, healthy and blessed days. 🤲🏻
